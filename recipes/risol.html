Make the Wrapper (Crepe): In a bowl, mix flour and salt. Add egg, water (or milk), and oil gradually while whisking until smooth. Strain the batter if lumpy. Heat a non-stick pan, pour a thin layer of batter and swirl it around to form a thin crepe. Cook for about 1 minute on low heat until it sets (no need to flip). Set aside on a plate and repeat. 2. Prepare the Filling: Mix mayonnaise, sweetened condensed milk (if using), and pepper. On each crepe, place a slice of boiled egg, some strips of smoked beef, and a spoonful of mayo mix. Sprinkle cheese if desired. 3. Fold and Roll: Fold the sides of the crepe inward, then roll from bottom to top tightly like a spring roll. Repeat for all wrappers. 4. Coat the Risol: Dip each rolled risol in beaten egg, then coat with breadcrumbs. 5. Deep Fry: Heat oil over medium heat. Fry the risol until golden brown and crispy. Drain on paper towels. ü•ó Serving Suggestion: Serve hot with chili sauce or bottled mayo-chili mix. Perfect for snacks, gatherings, or lunchbox fillers!
