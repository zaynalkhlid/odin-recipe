Risol Mayo

Ingredients:

For the Wrapper (Crepes):

  • 250 grams all-purpose flour
  • 1 egg
  • 500 ml water or milk
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil or melted butter
  • 1/2 tsp salt

For the Filling:

  • 4 boiled eggs, sliced
  • 150 grams smoked beef or chicken ham, cut into strips
  • 100 grams grated cheese (optional, for cheesy version)
  • 5-6 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp sweetened condensed milk (optional, for creamy sweetness)
  • A dash of pepper

For Coating and Frying:

  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Cooking oil for deep frying

Instructions:

  1. Make the Wrapper (Crepe):
    • In a bowl, mix flour and salt.
    • Add egg, water (or milk), and oil gradually while whisking until smooth.
    • Strain the batter if lumpy.
    • Heat a non-stick pan, pour a thin layer of batter and swirl it around to form a thin crepe.
    • Cook for about 1 minute on low heat until it sets (no need to flip).
    • Set aside on a plate and repeat.
  2. Prepare the Filling:
    • Mix mayonnaise, sweetened condensed milk (if using), and pepper.
    • On each crepe, place a slice of boiled egg, some strips of smoked beef, and a spoonful of mayo mix.
    • Sprinkle cheese if desired.
  3. Fold and Roll:
    • Fold the sides of the crepe inward, then roll from bottom to top tightly like a spring roll.
    • Repeat for all wrappers.
  4. Coat the Risol:
    • Dip each rolled risol in beaten egg, then coat with breadcrumbs.
  5. Deep Fry:
    • Heat oil over medium heat.
    • Fry the risol until golden brown and crispy.
    • Drain on paper towels.

Serving Suggestion:

  • Serve hot with chili sauce or bottled mayo-chili mix.
  • Perfect for snacks, gatherings, or lunchbox fillers!
